Basuki Hartono

About Artist

Basuki is an artist who creates abstract and expressive works. His paintings are highly detailed and visually striking; harnessing the geometric shape, Basuki’s pieces are an accomplished exploration into materiality, color, form and texture which push the boundaries of abstract painting. His work is reminiscent of visual phenomena, such as bokeh: the blurred, out of focus part of a photograph. He often works in layers to develop texture and depth in her works, mixing a bright palette to create an interesting dialogue between colors and textures.

Basuki uses existing objects around the studio to create his works, like upcycling old canvases and collaging her old artworks to create new ones. This sustainable act lends extremely well to Markus’s practice - previously discarded materials have become high quality artworks, with each old object creating an exciting surface for creative expression.
